Table pathways_us_2016-08-31.public.CleaningMethods
Cleaning methods are used to interpolate or extrapolate to missing years when data is entered. In particular, these methods are used first on demand drivers to fill in missing years out to the model end year. For example, population, a major demand driver, might be extrapolated from 2020 (last available estimate) to 2050 (model end year).
